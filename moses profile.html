Isaac Moses Etudaye is an Enthusiastic and passionate pianist and organist who’s music cut across classical, contemporary, gospel and choral music…
Has had quite a number of years of musicianship both in piano playing and organ playing also with choral works ..
He was a student of Muson basic school years back which he later sat for exams and did excellent..
He is currently An organist of ST JUDES ANGLICAN CHURCH OJOTA, And sub organist to other church on weekly basis.. Head accompanist of a few chorales and Assitant to others..
Had shared stage with the likes of Music Icons:

      Babajide Odedeji,
      Paul Awoyemi,
      Paul Awoyemi,
      Tosin Ajayi,
      Adejola Adeosun
    and many other reputable musician …
    • Had attempted quite a number of Piano works and little organ works …
